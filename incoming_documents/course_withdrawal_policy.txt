COURSE WITHDRAWAL POLICY
Office of the Registrar — Academic Year 2026

1. WITHDRAWAL DEADLINE
Students may withdraw from an individual course with a grade of "W" up until
the end of WEEK 10 of a standard 15-week semester. For 8-week accelerated
terms, the deadline is the end of WEEK 5. The exact calendar date is published
each term in the Academic Calendar.

2. HOW TO WITHDRAW
Withdrawals must be submitted through the Student Portal under
"Registration > Drop/Withdraw." Paper forms are no longer accepted except by
petition to the Registrar for documented emergencies.

3. FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS
- Withdrawing before the end of Week 2 results in a full tuition refund for
  that course.
- Withdrawing between Week 3 and the withdrawal deadline (Week 10) results in
  no refund, and a "W" grade is recorded (does not affect GPA).
- Withdrawing after the deadline requires a documented hardship petition and,
  if approved, results in a grade of "WF" which is calculated as an F in the
  cumulative GPA.

4. FULL WITHDRAWAL FROM THE UNIVERSITY
Students withdrawing from ALL courses in a term must additionally complete an
exit interview with their academic advisor and clear all financial holds
before the withdrawal is finalized.

5. IMPACT ON FINANCIAL AID AND SCHOLARSHIPS
Dropping below full-time enrollment (12 credit hours) as a result of a
withdrawal may affect scholarship eligibility and financial aid disbursement.
Students should consult the Office of Financial Aid before withdrawing.
